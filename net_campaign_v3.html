the card

A landscape.
A line.
That's enough.

The card is not a post. It's a coordinate — where your thinking is pointing right now. Post one every few days, whenever something shifts. The front is for anyone passing through. The back is for people who lean in.

The slot hole
Top corner of every card. Empty = passing by. Tap it — it fills blue. Your card is on their rope. A knot forms. No message needed.
The front — one line
The question on your mind this week. Not a caption. Not a headline. The actual thing. Rough is better than polished.
The back — flip to go deeper
Why now. The threads you're following. What kind of crossing you're open to. Only people genuinely pulled by your front will flip.
Stickers — attach your face
Spin the wheel, write one line, your face gets placed on their card. Tap a face stack to open that thread. Public sideways thinking.
